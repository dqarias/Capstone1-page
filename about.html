SAVE THE ANDEAN CAT WITH PATA PROJECT

The Andean cat is one of the rarest cats in the world. This mysterious animal lives exclusively in the Andes Mountains and Patagonia steppe, where climates are harsh and food is scarce. Sadly, due to human factors such as mining, hunting, and unregulated tourism, the Andean cat population is rapidly decreasingâ€”with estimates of fewer than 1,400 remaining today. Land conservation is critical to the survival of their species, because their habitat is isolated in the high desert of the Andes.
